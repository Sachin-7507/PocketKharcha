About PocketKharcha

PocketKharcha is your smart personal finance companion — helping you manage daily expenses, track spending, and stay on top of your financial goals with ease.

Whether it’s your tea breaks, travel bills, or monthly rent, PocketKharcha keeps everything organized in one place. With an easy-to-use interface, filtering, and tracking tools, you’ll always know where your money goes.

This app is designed with simplicity and clarity in mind — so you can focus more on life, and less on calculations.
